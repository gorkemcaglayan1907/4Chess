Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 4Chess: Battle of Four ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your location.

2. Leaderboard and Names

When you play a ranked match, the username you choose and your total score are stored on our server to display the global leaderboard. This information is public within the App but is not linked to your real identity. We do not store any device-specific identifiers (IMEI, IDFA, etc.).

3. Profile Photos

If you choose to upload a profile photo, it is stored on our server for the sole purpose of displaying it to other players during a match. We do not use this data for tracking or advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not include advertisements or third-party tracking SDKs. We do not share your data with any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Every player is treated with the same commitment to anonymity.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at: support@fourchess.onrender.com
